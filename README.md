## What Are AI Training Jobs?

AI training jobs are remote, flexible, freelance roles where you evaluate, rank, or create content to improve AI models. Common tasks include:

- **RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback)** — rating AI responses for quality, accuracy, and safety
- **Prompt engineering** — writing prompts and ideal responses to train LLMs
- **Code review** — evaluating AI-generated code for correctness and style
- **Data annotation** — labeling images, audio, or text for machine learning pipelines
- **Expert evaluation** — assessing AI outputs in specialized domains (medicine, law, finance, etc.)

Pay ranges from $10/hr for general tasks to $400+/hr for niche expert roles (physicians, PhDs, senior engineers).
