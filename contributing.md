# Contributing

This is a curated list — quality over quantity.

## Guidelines

- The resource must be genuinely useful to someone getting into or advancing in AI training, RLHF, or data annotation work.
- Check it's not already listed before submitting.
- Platforms must be actively hiring (not defunct or paused indefinitely).
- Guides and tools must be free or have a meaningful free tier.

## How to contribute

1. Fork the repo
2. Add your entry at the bottom of the appropriate section
3. Follow the format: `- [Name](url) - One-sentence description ending with a period.`
4. Open a pull request with a short explanation of why it belongs

## What we don't accept

- Duplicate entries
- Self-promotional content without genuine value
- Paid-only resources without a free tier
- Platforms with no public reviews or verifiable track record
